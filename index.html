Test Your Memory!

Flip the cards, match all pairs, and sharpen your memory while having fun.

About

This game is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It challenges your brain to recall positions and test your concentration skills. Created as a fun project to sharpen logic and design skills.

Rules

  • Click on two cards to flip them over.
  • If the cards match, they stay flipped.
  • If not, they flip back after a short delay.
  • Match all pairs to win the game.
